Who is Beatriz?

Pronouns She/Her

I like to call myself a Jane-of-all-trades. My curious brain (and a little bit of ADHD) guides me through finding pleasure in knowledge - whether it is learning new bass songs, cooking new recipes, pushing the limits of how big an H1 can be...

I have three tattoos so far: a big one on the inside of my arm, a smaller one on my wrist and another on my ribs. My 2025 plan is to finish part of my arm sleeve. Click here to find my tattoo inspiration.

My music taste is really diverse. I listen to jazz, heavy metal and drum and bass all in the same day, sometimes even in the same hour! My favourite band is (well was...) Nirvana, and KoRn.

I play some videogames, but I tend to always go back to the vintage ones - you can find me spending hours just playing The Sims Bustin Out on GBA.

Life was a highway, and I rode it all night long

*Queue Tom Cochrane’s music*

With over one decade of working, I’ve had really different jobs - all ranging from Customer Service/reception, bartending, to working in a Casino serving drinks and also (finally) as a Designer. Even though my design path only caught up five years ago, I still take all the experience and use it to improve myself professionally.
