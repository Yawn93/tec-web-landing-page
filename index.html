Who is Beatriz?

Pronouns She/Her

I like to call myself a Jane-of-all-trades. My curious brain (and a little bit of ADHD) guides me through finding pleasure in knowledge - whether it is learning new bass songs, cooking new recipes, pushing the limits of how big an H1 can be...

I have three tattoos so far: a big one on the inside of my arm, a smaller one on my wrist and another on my ribs. My 2025 plan is to finish part of my arm sleeve. Click here to find my tattoo inspiration.

My music taste is really diverse. I listen to jazz, heavy metal and drum and bass all in the same day, sometimes even in the same hour! My favourite band is (well was...) Nirvana, and KoRn.

I play some videogames, but I tend to always go back to the vintage ones - you can find me spending hours just playing The Sims Bustin Out on GBA.

Ink, sleep, repeat

I still find fascinating how I can be afraid of getting vaccine shots, and spend hours laying down with a tattoo needle on my arm. But hey, I myself am a contradition!

My first tattoo was done when I was 21 years old, and it happen when I got my first paycheck (sweeeeet financial freedom!). It’s an homage to my mother - a reminder from her to always be Wise. The second one was done several years later. I always wanted to have Nirvana lyrics tattoed on me so I did just that. My third one was a masterpiece (well for me). I wanted to have Lou Reed’s lyrics on my body as well (see the common thread here?), but the artist had another idea: a mix of lyrics with a woman drawing. I love looking at that tattoo, and I hope to keep it forever.

I believe that tattoos shouldn’t be a matter of discrimination on the workplace. I worked with so many colleagues that were professionally light-years ahead of me, and were tattoed. But hey, let’s keep pushing!
